About Lauren

Life is a journey of constant growth and change. But sometimes, we hit a point where the path forward feels unclear, and we feel stuck, uncertain, or overwhelmed. I believe we all have the strength to navigate these moments, and my role is to offer the guidance and support to help you find your way.

You don’t have to navigate this alone. If you're ready to move from feeling stuck to feeling empowered, I invite you to reach out for a free consultation.

Common Challenges

Are you or your family experiencing one of these challenges?

  • Navigating the heavy weight of grief or loss.
  • Struggling to find effective coping strategies for stress and anxiety.
  • Feeling overwhelmed by challenges with focus, organization, or motivation (executive functioning).
  • Supporting a child or teen through social challenges or school difficulties.
  • Seeking a deeper sense of self‑discovery and purpose in your own life.
  • Wanting to incorporate mindfulness to feel more present and calm.

Therapeutic Approach

As a former school counselor, I have a unique understanding of the pressures faced by children, teens, and their families. My approach is solution‑focused, meaning our work together will be collaborative and forward‑looking. We won’t just talk about the problems; we will actively build on your strengths to develop practical skills and create the positive changes you want to see in your life.
